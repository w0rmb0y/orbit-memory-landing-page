How Orbit works

Orbit turns journaling from a task into an atmosphere.

The core experience is built around one feeling: users are not filling in forms, tracking productivity, or doing emotional homework. They are placing thoughts into the sky and watching their inner world become visible over time.

✎

Write

Drop in a full journal entry or a single sentence when that’s all you’ve got.

◉

Speak

Record thoughts naturally with voice journaling and automatic transcription.

⌾

Remember

Add photos to anchor a feeling, memory, or moment in real life.

◌

Check in

Log quick moods when words feel heavy, messy, or just not happening today.

The visual system

Your journal becomes a personal galaxy, not a pile of forgotten notes.

Orbit gives memories shape. Entries can drift, cluster, glow, and connect based on emotional tone, importance, and recurring themes. The result feels intimate and calming while still helping users notice what keeps returning in their lives.

☁

Clouds for passing thoughts

Small reflections, in-between feelings, daily noise, and the texture of ordinary days.

✦

Stars for meaningful moments

Moments that matter stand out naturally and glow with more emotional weight.

☾

Constellations for life stories

Related memories connect across time, turning scattered entries into visible personal narratives.

◎

An evolving Orbit Map

As users journal, memories form a navigable galaxy of themes, milestones, and emotional patterns.

☼

Emotional Weather

Clearer skies can reflect calm periods. Cloudier skies can hint at stress or uncertainty. Brighter stars can signal joy. It stays soft and atmospheric, never clinical.

✧

AI that reflects, not lectures

Orbit’s AI acts like a quiet reflection guide. It notices patterns, asks gentle questions, and helps users revisit memories without sounding preachy or robotic.

❍

A memory archive that feels alive

Over time, Orbit becomes part journal, part story map, part emotional archive. Users can look back and literally see their inner universe take shape.
